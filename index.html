        <h3>About the Community Design Jams</h3>

        <p>After the spring series, each community partner presents a real challenge they need help solving. Participants—students, neighbors, professionals, creatives—form teams and join a two-week design sprint. The process starts asynchronously and culminates in a <strong>live hybrid pitch session</strong> to community stakeholders. Teams must pitch to be eligible for prizes.</p>

        <p>Each Jam is supported by a multidisciplinary Jefferson student <strong>Jam Leader</strong> who guides teams, connects them with community partners and resources, and keeps momentum throughout the sprint.</p>
